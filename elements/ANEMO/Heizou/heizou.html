Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Swirl focused playstyle that synergizes well with Anemo.

• Above average base ATK for a 4-Star character.

• Can quickly create Declension stacks through swirled Normal Attacks, letting him deal max Elemental Skill DMG.

Character Weaknesses

• Most of Heizou's damage comes from his Elemental Skill, so failing to CRIT with it or worse, missing it or being stunned immensely lowers his DPS.

• Elemental Burst explodes on the first enemy hit, making it require proper positioning.

• Heizou is an Elemental Reaction enabler that requires equally invested off-field teammates.
