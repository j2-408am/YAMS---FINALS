Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Constant Cryo usage useful for Elemental Reactions such as Freeze due to being a catalyst user.

• Relies on Normal Attacks for big and constant DMG, which lessens dependence on Elemental Skill and Burst CD.

• High DMG scaling and potential for Melt.

• Capable of self-healing should his HP fall too low.

Character Weaknesses

• Normal Attack AoE is relatively small; better suited against bosses than mobs, unless you can group those enemies together.

• Without C1, his Special Charged Attack and self-healing has a 5s cooldown and cannot be activated efficiently with certain healers.
