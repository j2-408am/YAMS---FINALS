Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Great off-field Electro damage and application especially on Aggravate or Quicken teams.

• Elemental Skill totems have wide targeting range and may hit any enemies in the vicinity. It also hits foes randomly, which can be an advantageous perk if you're battling against enemies that are spread out.

Character Weaknesses

• Elemental Burst has an extremely high energy cost just for extra damage. It also removes the totems, which requires you to set them up again.

• Setup for placing her Elemental Skill require more on-field time, which may lead to team rotation issues.

• Elemental Skill totems may unintentionally hit objects and animals in the overworld.
