Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Kaeya's short cooldowns and energy requirements allow for easy building of a reliable party member.

• Capable of functioning as both a decent Cryo and Physical DPS due to his scalings.

• Good off-field Cryo applications allow Kaeya to have seamless transitions in most teams.

Character Weaknesses

• Elemental Skill and Burst has a small area of effect, favoring a Melee DPS unit if it is not Kaeya.

• Kaeya's Constellations are more rare than most 4-stars.
