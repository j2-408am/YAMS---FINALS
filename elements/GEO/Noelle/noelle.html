Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Stacking DEF increases her damage, shielding, and healing all at once.

• Wide AoE attacks when using Charged Attacks.

• Healing is party-wide and not only for the active character.

• Emergency shielding when active character goes below 30%.

Character Weaknesses

• Low Base DMG when not on C6.

• Elemental Skill has a high cooldown, and not ideal for panic shielding or healing.

• Has to be on-field to heal your party, especially if built only as a support.

• High Elemental Burst energy cost.
