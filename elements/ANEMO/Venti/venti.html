Character Strengths

• Regenerates Energy fast to use his burst off-cooldown.

• Best Crowd Control for lighter enemies with Elemental Burst.

• Can fit in any team comp if AoE crowd control is required.

• Elemental Skill creates a wind current for exploration.

Character Weaknesses

• Elemental Burst is not good against heavier enemies or single-target bosses.
