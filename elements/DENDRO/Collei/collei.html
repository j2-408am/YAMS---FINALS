Collei

★★★★

Originally an Eleazar-afflicted victim of Fatui human experimentation, Collei wandered the world as a vagrant until she met Amber in Mondstadt. Since then, Collei has turned her life around, and currently serves as a trainee Forest Ranger in the Avidya Forest.

Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Elemental Skill can trigger Quicken and Spread reaction on its own.

• Elemental Burst has a wide area of effect.

• Kit offers variety in Dendro Application.

• Applies more Dendro compared to Dendro MC, which can be beneficial in Bloom teams.

Character Weaknesses

• Long Elemental Skill Cooldown.

• Elemental Burst applies Dendro only a few times.

• Weak Normal Attack multipliers.

• Elemental Skill locks you in a brief animation which can leave you vulnerable.
