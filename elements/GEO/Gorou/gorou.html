Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Boosts DEF, interruption resistance, and Geo DMG, making him the premier support for Geo-focused teams.

• Buffs the entire party's DEF after using Elemental Burst, and damage in the case of DEF-scaling characters.

• C4 can heal active characters during Elemental Burst, turning Gorou into a viable healer in his otherwise healerless comps.

Character Weaknesses

• Almost all of his potential is locked behind Mono Geo, severely limiting his possible comp choices.

• Elemental Burst has a high Energy Cost, making him energy-hungry if ran anywhere else but Mono Geo.
