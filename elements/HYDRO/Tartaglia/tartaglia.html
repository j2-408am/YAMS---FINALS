Character Strengths

• Melee stance and Riptide provide incredibly fast Hydro application to set up reactions like Vaporize or Electro-Charged.

• Riptide damage increases exponentially the more enemies there are.

• High damage scaling on the multipliers of his Elemental Burst.

Character Weaknesses

• Rotation requires careful planning and cooldown management to master.

• Cooldown reduction is locked behind constellations.
