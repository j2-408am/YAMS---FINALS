Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Elemental Skill deals off-field Dendro damage while healing the active character.

• C1 provides a Dendro DMG Buff.

• Dendro Resistance from her Elemental Burst helps a lot in reducing self-inflicted Dendro Core damage.

• Passive talent lets you sneak up on some wildlife.

Character Weaknesses

• High energy cost for her Elemental Burst.

• Elemental Skill does not heal off-field characters.

• Swapping out of Yaoyao's Elemental Burst state will end it early and remove the summoned Yuguei's.

• Damage and Healing split scales with ATK and HP respectively.
