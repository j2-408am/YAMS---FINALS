Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Elemental Burst allows him to take advantage of his high multipliers on his Plunging Attacks.

• Great AoE coverage, often being one of the best picks against a large wave of enemies.

• Elemental Skill and passive talent makes it easier to explore mountainous regions like Liyue.

Character Weaknesses

• Reliant on his Elemental Burst to be used every rotation, but he has a relatively high energy cost. While under his Elemental Burst, it also disables the energy particle generation on his Skill. He will need more Energy Recharge than usual unless you have triple Anemo team comps or an abundance of Energy Recharge weapons on your support.

• His fragile playstyle may be a turn-off for some players who wants a safer pick, as he has no way to sustain himself and is susceptible to interruptions.

• Plunges can push back lighter enemies, which may spread enemies farther away from your position.
