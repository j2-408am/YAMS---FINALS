Diluc Ragnvindr is the owner of Mondstadt's Dawn Winery and Angel's Share Tavern. As a former Knight of Favonius, Diluc is skilled in both combat and commerce.

Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Can trigger repeat Elemental Reaction with constant Pyro infusion via his Elemental Skill and Burst, allowing him to reliable trigger Vaporize and Melt.

• Elemental Burst has low ER requirements and low cooldown, ensuring almost constant Pyro Infusion uptime.

• Ascends with CRIT Rate, making it easier to build him as your Main DPS.

Character Weaknesses

• Low Attack Speed, this may be an issue with supports with limited buff times.

• Lacks a weapon that he can perfectly synergize with, compared to other 5-Star Main-DPS.
