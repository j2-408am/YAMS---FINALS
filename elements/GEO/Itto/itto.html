Arataki Itto

★★★★★

A loud and proud descendant of the crimson oni, Itto is also the leader and founder of the Arataki Gang. While not a villain, his reputation as a delinquent in Inazuma City leaves something to be desired.

Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• High sustained Charged Attack DMG when enhanced with Superstrength Stacks from Normal Attacks & Elemental Skill.

• Ushi, his Elemental Skill, can deal significant damage and be easily be used and integrated into his Superstrength Stacks combos.

Character Weaknesses

• High investment is needed to activate his high-cost Elemental Burst every rotation. In addition, he requires teams (especially with Gorou) to enable his full potential, which means he is not flexible as he's holed up in Mono-Geo teams.

• Elemental Burst cancels when immediately switched out during its duration, which limits the supports he can pair with.
