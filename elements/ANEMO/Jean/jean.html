Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Elemental Skill can be used to toss enemies into the air, allowing them to take fall damage. This works even in the abyss.

• Healing capabilities scale off ATK, incentivizing building her as a respectable Sub-DPS.

• Ascension passive drastically lowers her ER requirements due to its energy refunding.

• Elemental Burst provides AoE Burst Heal and DMG. This can serve as a huge benefit for Furina teams, as Jean can easily stack Fanfare points and shred resistance if required.

Character Weaknesses

• Elemental Burst has a long cooldown, and can sometimes limit the player from the most utilized aspect of her kit.

• Elemental Burst's AoE is stationary, making her less effective against fast-moving opponents that reposition often.

• May push enemies away from each other during her combos.
