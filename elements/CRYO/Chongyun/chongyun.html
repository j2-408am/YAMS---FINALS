Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Enables niche Cryo infusion builds for the party.

• One of the only few characters who can buff ATK SPD.

• Low Elemental Burst energy cost for quick swap Burst.

Character Weaknesses

• Slow Normal Attacks as a Main DPS.

• Area of Effect on his Skill is small.

• Few characters benefit from his infusion meta-wise.

• Claymore disrupts Freeze reaction outside of Shatter builds.
