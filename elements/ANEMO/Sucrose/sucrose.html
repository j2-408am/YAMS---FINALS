Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• As a catalyst user, Sucrose is able to apply Anemo with ease, making her an effective on-field driver for Swirl.

• Is able to increase the Elemental Mastery of the party through ability usage, making her an extremely formidable support.

• Is as effective as her premier 5-start counterparts if built and played correctly.

• C1 gives her more Crowd Control and RES shred opportunities, as well as making her rotations more flexible.

Character Weaknesses

• Mechanically intensive to play, requiring the player to understand the fundamentals of her Elemental Infusion to use her effectively.

• Long cooldowns with a short duration unless using Sacrificial Fragments or getting her C1 constellation.
