Bennett

★★★★

An orphan discovered by an elderly adventurer as a baby, Bennett was raised in Mondstadt's Adventurers' Guild. He is the one and only member of "Benny's Adventure Team," as everyone else left the team after experiencing the constant misfortune that follows him.

Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• One of the best ATK buffers in the game that also provides fast healing and energy at a low cost.

• Can accumulate large amounts of energy with his low-cooldown Elemental Skill, making his Elemental Burst spammable.

• Can easily apply Pyro to set-up reactions. The burst also provides a field of Pyro that can be used for applying reactions in Sunfire builds or Kazuha's swirl.

Character Weaknesses

• Fully charged Elemental Skill outside of Burst field causes knockback.
