Kujou Sara

★★★★

A tengu, Sara is the adopted daughter of the Kujou Clan of the Tenryou Commission. Loyal to both her clan and the Shogun, Sara carries out her orders by the Shogun's will.

Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Huge ATK buffs comparable to Bennett's buffing.

• Both Elemental Skill and Burst applies the same buffs, giving her two ways to buff the party.

• Landing your Elemental Skill restores energy to the whole party.

Character Weaknesses

• Her Elemental Skill can be awkward to use, and it'll only be eased if you get her automatic buffing at C2.

• Buff only lasts for 6 seconds.

• Extremely high Energy Recharge requirement.

• Has less support abilities compared to others like Bennett.
