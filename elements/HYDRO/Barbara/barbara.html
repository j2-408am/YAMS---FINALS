Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Elemental Skill can burst heal or heal over time.

• Can auto revive characters at C6, which gives Barbara the benefit of having a rare safety net.

• As a catalyst user, her hydro application is incredibly consistent, allowing her to slot into Dendro-focused teams as an on-field driver for Bloom and Hyperbloom.

Character Weaknesses

• One-trick pony character, as most of her kit only revolves around Hydro application and healing.

• Skills have long cooldowns, giving her an extremely limited time where she is effective if not played on-field.

• Applies the Wet status on to herself and her teammates when casting her Elemental Skill, which may lead to enemies triggering reactions against you.
