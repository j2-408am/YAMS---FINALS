Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Piercing DMG gives Sethos a good avenue to deal multi-target damage, even allowing him to hit Bloom Cores in the arrow's flight path.

• Elemental Skill gives Sethos a swift exit out of bad positions and regenerates Elemental Energy to be used by his Empowered Shots.

• Scales on Elemental Mastery and is an Electro unit, granting him access to Aggravate and Hyperbloom in his playstyles

Character Weaknesses

• Energy-hungry due to how quickly he eats up Elemental Energy, requiring an Electro battery to keep him topped up.

• Level 2 Charged Shot forces Sethos to become immobile, rendering him susceptible to interruption and damage.

• The Charged Shots cost some of Sethos' Elemental Energy, delaying the usage of his next combo if he cannot recover it.
