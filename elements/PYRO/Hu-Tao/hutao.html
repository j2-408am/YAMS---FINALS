Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• HP conversion to ATK contributes a huge amount of boost to her already good scaling and Vaporize potential.

• Monstrous DPS against boss-type enemies or in single target situations.

• Elemental Burst enables her to be self-sufficient especially with her high-risk, high-reward playstyle.

• Provides CRIT Rate for other party members.

Character Weaknesses

• High damage potential requires her to have low HP, making her best performances incredibly risky without a shielder, healer, or her Elemental Burst.

• Often struggles against wild groups of enemies as she lacks AoE attacks outside of Elemental Burst or Xianyun teams.

• Fast application of the Pyro element reduces her best team comp choices to make Vaporize more consistent for her, often necessitating either Xingqiu or Yelan.

• Unless you have her C1, limited stamina can dampen her performance and smoothness for Charged Attacks spam playstyle.
