Character Reviews

Character Strengths

• Large AoE on Elemental Skill and Burst.

• Can drive multiple Elemental Reactions and effects with Normal Attacks..

• Good F2P option for dendro-focused teams.

• Elemental Burst decreases the DEF resistance of enemies, which is a rarity.

Character Weaknesses

• High Elemental Burst energy cost. Investing in Energy Recharge sacrifices her potential for greater damage.

• Holding down the Elemental Skill makes Lisa vulnerable to attacks.

• Hard to get constellations.
